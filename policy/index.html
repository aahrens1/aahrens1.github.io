I work with public agencies and international organisations to bring causal inference and machine learning to bear on policy questions, mainly in migration and labour-market policy.

Path2Work

My current focus is ↪Path2Work, an online job-matching platform that supports refugees in Switzerland in finding employment. Path2Work is built jointly with a team of researchers at ETH Zurich and the University of Lausanne, and is supported by the Swiss State Secretariat for Migration (SEM) and the Swiss State Secretariat for Economic Affairs (SECO), in partnership with UNHCR.

Previous project partners

  • International Organization for Migration (IOM) — impact evaluation of cash-based interventions for Venezuelan refugees in Peru
  • State Secretariat for Economic Affairs (SECO), Switzerland — evaluation of the Swiss job vacancy notice obligation
  • Central Statistics Office, Ireland — review of methodologies for owner-occupier housing indices
  • Environmental Protection Agency, Ireland — research on land use and spatial planning
  • Residential Tenancies Board, Ireland — analysis of rent pressure zones and rental-price inflation
